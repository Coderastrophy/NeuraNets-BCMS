Our Story

Founded in 2024, NeuraNotes emerged from a simple frustration: existing note-taking apps were either too simple or too complex. We wanted to create something that feels natural, yet powerful enough to handle the complexity of modern knowledge work. Today, we're helping thousands of people capture, organize, and amplify their ideas.
